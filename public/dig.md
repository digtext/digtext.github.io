- Writing dig text is simple: use bulleted lists and every sub-bullet creates a dig.
    - Hit tab or shift tab to indent or outdent text.
- You can work in your own editor and just paste the bulleted list here.
    - You can paste any bullet format, for example `-`, `*`, or `+`, also indented text works
- ( Why bulleted list? (( Bulleted lists are the best dig-writing experience. They let you see the entire structure clearly. Plus you can do it in any text editor. )) ).
